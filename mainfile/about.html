About

GameStudio is a final project for the course CSC200 - Data Structures and Algorithms. This interactive web-based platform is made to showcase the students' understanding and visualization of fundamental DSA concepts through engaging games and puzzles. Each game implements specific data structures like stacks, queues, graphs, and algorithms such as BFS, recursion, backtracking, and Dijkstra's algorithm.
